Our Vision

Our vision is to develop the Treatment Planning Platform for Cancer Radiotherapy as a free, accessible resource for cancer patients and healthcare providers. We aim to empower patients with crucial information about radiotherapy treatment planning, making cutting-edge knowledge available to all, regardless of financial means. This platform is designed to offer support, education, and downloadable assets, ensuring that every patient has access to valuable resources that can improve their treatment journey and quality of life.
