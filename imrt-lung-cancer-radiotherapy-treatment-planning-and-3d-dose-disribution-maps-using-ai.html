The Intensity-Modulated Radiation Therapy (IMRT) model for lung cancer treatment planning leverages advanced artificial intelligence (AI) technologies to enhance precision and efficacy in radiotherapy. This model is designed to optimize treatment plans by delivering targeted radiation doses to lung tumors while minimizing exposure to surrounding healthy tissues.
